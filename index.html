Website Design

The visual layout and style of your website is important. There are many aspects that must be thought about during the planning phase such as branding, strategy, and appealing content, but a website also needs to serve as a business function because it is ultimately where potential customers, partners, employees, or investors get their initial impression. That impression may either keep them coming back or turn them away for good. I believe a website should be simple and pleasing to look at while serving its purpose with a layout that makes it easy for the end-user to find necessary information, interact with business resources, and, ultimately, promote the interests of the owning organization.

Website Optimization

Using good practices and anlytics to increase traffic to your website is the best way to spread your message and introduce new users to your products. Analytics are important becuase one can view statistical reports, analyze activity, and find mistakes, which ultimately lead to product enhancements. Below are some common technical terms used in website optimization.

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • User Experience (UX)
  • User Interface (UI)
  • Web Analytics
  • Web Governance
